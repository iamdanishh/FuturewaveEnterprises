The U.S. E-Commerce Landscape

The U.S. e-commerce market is one of the largest and fastest-growing industries globally. In 2023, online retail sales reached approximately $1.119 trillion, accounting for nearly 22% of total retail sales. This number is projected to grow significantly, with online sales expected to surpass $1.6 trillion by 2028, making up 28% of the total U.S. retail market. This growth is driven by changing consumer habits, the convenience of online shopping, and the increasing preference for doorstep delivery.
